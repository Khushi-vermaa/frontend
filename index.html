COMMON QUESTIONS AND HELPFUL ANSWERS

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by writing an email to us or contacting our sales team. For assistance, call us at +91 8595852495 or write to us at info@synergiapac.com for any queries or support.

What types of packaging do you offer?

We offer a variety of packaging solutions, including premium boxes and monocartons, custom-printed labels, flexible pouches, and durable laminated tubes. Tailored for industries like cosmetics, food, and pharmaceuticals, our packaging is customisable with high-quality printing, eco-friendly materials, and special finishes to enhance your product's functionality and brand appeal.

What is the typical turn-around time for orders?

Our typical turn-around time for orders is 5-7 business days. However, this timeline may vary depending on the size, complexity, and level of customisation required for your order. For highly customised packaging or larger quantities, additional time may be needed to ensure the highest quality standards are met. We also offer expedited services for urgent projects, subject to feasibility. Once we receive your order details, we will provide a clear and accurate timeline to meet your specific requirements. Our team is committed to delivering your packaging promptly while maintaining exceptional quality

How do you determine pricing for custom packaging?

Our custom packaging pricing is based on size, material choice (e.g., recycled paper or biodegradable films), design complexity, and order quantity. Special finishes or advanced printing may also affect costs. Contact us with your project details for a tailored quote!

Do you provide design services for custom packaging?

Yes, our expert design team collaborates closely with you to bring your custom packaging ideas to life, from concept creation to production-ready artwork. Additionally, our partnerships with leading design studios and advertising agencies give us a creative edge, ensuring packaging that not only aligns with your brand but also elevates it to stand out in the market. Leveraging advanced technologies like the HP Indigo 6K, we deliver stunning, high-quality designs that make a lasting impact.

Are your packaging products environmentally friendly?

At Synergia Pac, we offer a wide range of environmentally friendly substrates, enabling brands to produce high-quality, sustainable packaging. Options include recycled paper and cardstock, FSC-certified papers from responsibly managed forests, and biodegradable films like PLA. Recyclable materials such as mono-plastics, sugarcane bagasse paper, and durable Kraft paper offer versatile, eco-conscious solutions. Innovative substrates like stone paper, made from calcium carbonate, and PVC-free materials further enhance sustainability. Additionally, recycled PET (rPET) films provide flexible, durable packaging options. These substrates ensure vibrant prints while meeting the growing demand for environmentally friendly packaging.

What should I do if I receive a damaged package?

If you receive a damaged package, we understand how disappointing that can be and are here to help. Please reach out to our customer service executive at +91 8595852495 within 48 hours of receiving your order, and we will work with you to resolve the issue quickly, whether through a return or a replacement.

What materials are your packaging products made from?

Our packaging solutions are crafted using a range of materials specifically chosen for their compatibility with the HP Indigo 6K digital press. These include premium cardboard and paper for high-quality prints, as well as biodegradable options like PLA films and sugarcane bagasse paper, ensuring both vibrant designs and sustainability. We also offer recyclable materials and eco-conscious substrates like Kraft paper and FSC-certified paper, meeting the demands of environmentally responsible packaging without compromising on precision and quality.
